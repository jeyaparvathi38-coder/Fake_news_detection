The Core Engine

At the heart of NewsShield is a state-of-the-art DistilBERT Transformer. This isn't just a simple keyword matcher; it's a deep neural network capable of reading news articles exactly like a human does—understanding context, sarcasm, and sentence structure.

When you submit an article, the model first cleans the raw text, breaking it down into mathematical tokens. Then, its Self-Attention layers scan every word simultaneously to find hidden deceptive linguistic patterns that are invisible to the naked eye.

Trained specifically on an extensive Indian Fake News Database containing over 40,000 verified articles, the model achieves a staggering 99.9% accuracy in separating truth from misinformation.
